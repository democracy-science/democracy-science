Researchers in Turkey identify decreased vaccine immune response, recommend booster at months 6-12
Project supported by the Scientific Research Projects Office of the University of Cukurova, Turkey discovered that antibody concentrations after vaccination with two doses were found to be decreased. A booster at months 6-12 is recommended for the inactive SARS-CoV-2 vaccination.
