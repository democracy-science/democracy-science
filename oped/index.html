The Dangerous Push to Give Boosters to Teens
December 21, 2021
The emergence of the Omicron variant urged the need for boosters, however, underlying data didn’t necessarily support that it was time for booster shots to be FDA approved for teens 16 and up when it was announced.
We don't need universal booster shots. We need to reach the unvaccinated.
November 29, 2021
Although we see a push for boosters, the only people it really benefits are those that are high-risk, however, it is not a preventative against Delta, according to studies done. Current vaccines will do their part in preventing severe disease caused by the omicron variant, however, the booster will likely not be helpful in dealing with resistance against the variant. And these shots can be better used to help the unvaccinated.
